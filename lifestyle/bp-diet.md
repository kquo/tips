## Blood Pressure Diet
Blood pressure diet cheat-sheet.

### Quick Tips
- Choose fresh or minimally processed foods.  
- Rinse canned beans/vegetables to reduce sodium.  
- Use herbs, spices, lemon, and vinegar instead of salt.  
- Aim for mostly home-cooked meals.  

### ✔ Foods That Help Lower or Prevent High Blood Pressure

#### Potassium-Rich Foods
- Bananas  
- Oranges & citrus  
- Avocados  
- Tomatoes  
- Beans & lentils  
- Sweet potatoes  
- Spinach, kale, Swiss chard  

#### Vegetables & Greens
- Beets / beet juice  
- Arugula  
- Broccoli  
- Mixed vegetables  

#### Whole Grains
- Oats  
- Quinoa  
- Whole-grain breads & cereals  

#### Healthy Fats
- Olive oil  
- Nuts & seeds  
- Salmon, sardines, mackerel  

#### Low-Fat Dairy
- Yogurt  
- Kefir  
- Low-fat milk  


### ✖ Foods to Limit or Avoid

#### High-Sodium Foods
- Canned soups  
- Frozen meals  
- Fast food  
- Deli meats, bacon, sausage  
- Soy sauce, teriyaki sauce  

#### Processed Snacks
- Chips  
- Instant noodles  
- Packaged baked goods  

#### Sugary Foods & Drinks
- Soda  
- Desserts  
- Sweetened coffee drinks  

#### Excess Alcohol & Caffeine
- More than 1–2 drinks/day  
- Excess energy drinks or coffee (if BP-sensitive)  

#### Unhealthy Fats
- Fried foods  
- Processed meats  
- Foods containing trans fats
